Welcome to ITS Fencing Club

Join us

Sporting one of Ireland's very few fencing clubs, ITS fencing club has a rich and exciting history. The club is one of the oldest, formed in 1985, it has seen many champions and has won many awards for it's world leading athletes. Open to players of all levels, the club offers a host of different events, from introductory workshops, to sparring tournaments for our more experiences members.
