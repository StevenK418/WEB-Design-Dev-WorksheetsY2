Welcome to ITS Cricket Club

Join us

IT Sligo's cricket club was started in 1994 from humble beginnings. Over the next 3 years, the club would garner more and more interest with students joining from all over the world and from every kind of background imaginable. The club provides an even playing field for players both new and experienced. Whatever your playing level, ITS Cricket club will allow you to discover your new found skills and play at both a casual and competitive level. Whetever your level of interest is, by joining our club, you will your experience with us a great way to relax, engage and meet new people over one of the most popular sports in history.
